ABOUT ME

Experienced Multimedia Artist | Blending Art & Tech for Captivating Creations

Hi, I’m Jheff Mathew Alcaraz, a 23-year-old multimedia artist based in Quezon City, Philippines. I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Computer Science (BSCS) from the University of the East (UE), where I was honored as a Cum Laude Latin awardee. Despite my technical background, my true passion has always been in multimedia arts, which I pursued to combine my love for creativity with my technical skills.

With over a year and a half of experience, I specialize in crafting visually engaging designs, immersive storytelling, and dynamic 3D content and animations. I thrive on blending creativity and technology to bring concepts to life—whether through graphic design, video editing, animation, or 3D modeling.

When I’m not creating, you’ll find me exploring new trends in design, honing my skills, and seeking inspiration to elevate my craft. I’m always eager to collaborate and bring fresh perspectives to every project.
